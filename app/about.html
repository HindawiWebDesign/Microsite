Robust Publishing Ethics

Hindawi is founded on an ethos of openness, we are passionate about Open Access Publishing. We are focused on our users while preserving excellence in our output and breadth of expertise. This openness is built around inclusion and the pursuit of knowledge from every corner of the world.
